Metal Assemblies Services

Openex Metal Fab is an industry leader in producing various high-quality metal parts and components to meet the demands of our customers. From riveting components together to providing complex welded assemblies, we offer excellent metal assembly services in addition to custom roll forming to serve the needs of clients in a wide range of industries.

When you need metal assembly services, it's absolutely essential that they are performed to your exact specifications. With our dedicated and skilled team offering assembly services, we can take the specifications you provide us and return the exact metal components you are looking for.

Openex Metal Fab has the knowledge and experience needed to be your metal components manufacturer of choice.

For more than 20 years, Openex Metal Fab has been a trusted provider of metal assembly services for a wide range of industries. We utilize Bills of Materials (BOMs) for every phase of the assembly process. This enables us to ensure consistency, quality, and compliance with design and engineering specifications in all of our manufacturing and assembly operations.

When you partner with Openex Metal Fab, you gain access to a manufacturer that is dedicated to the success of your product. With a team of highly skilled experts and facilities equipped with the most advanced manufacturing technology, we aim to deliver innovative metal assembly services that will help you get your product to market faster.

Comprehensive Assembly Solutions

At Openex Metal Fab, we're proud to provide full manufacturing services to meet all of your metal parts production needs.   • SCALABILITY: With metal assembly services, we can scale your production up or down as needed without the need for disruptions.
