Metal Fabrication Service

Openex Metal Fabrication has specialized in large-scale, complex, steel industrial fabrication and contract manufacturing for over two decades.

Our team of highly skilled fabricators and machinists has many years of experience manufacturing heavy industrial components. Openex has built a reputation for delivering the highest quality work on time and within budget. Some of the equipment we manufacture includes material handling systems, power generation equipment, structural steel components, and more.

Openex Metal Fabrication offers various industrial fabrication services, including professional end-to-end project management, Design for Manufacturability (DFM) review and assistance, custom engineering, and detailing. We also offer machining, surface preparation and painting, third-party quality testing services, as well as a wholesale metal supply division.

What Makes Us Different

Openex Metal Fabrication is a custom industrial fabricator offering a full range of contract manufacturing services. Openex has built a solid reputation as a high-quality contract manufacturer, and industrial fabricator with the capability to install large components and sub-assemblies since the company's founding.

Our full-service manufacturing solution for built-to-order projects include:

  • Large-Scale Industrial Metal Fabrication
  • Machining - Including very large-scale
  • Surface Preparation, Blasting, and Painting
  • Assembly
  • Quality Testing Services
  • Wholesale Metal Supply
