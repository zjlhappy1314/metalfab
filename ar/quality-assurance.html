Quality Assurance

At Openex, quality is non-negotiable. Every product we deliver has passed a rigorous process of inspection, testing, and verification to ensure full compliance with all specifications and customer requirements.

From the moment raw materials arrive to the final stages of production, our Quality Department conducts thorough inspections throughout the entire manufacturing process. Nothing leaves our facility unless it meets our strict standards.

Each customer order is treated as a dedicated project. A specialized Quality Inspector team is assigned to oversee every step, backed by a tailored Quality Plan developed specifically for the job.

Certified Inspectors

Our inspection personnel are fully qualified and certified for the relevant specialties. This includes:

  • AWS Certified Welding Inspectors
  • NDT Inspectors certified in Radiographic Testing (RT) and other methods

In-House Testing Capabilities

  • 3D Laser Measuring (Laser Tracker)
