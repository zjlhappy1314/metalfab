Custom Fabrication of Flotation Machine Components

Precision Steel Fabrication for Mining & Processing Plants

At Openex Metal Fabrication, we manufacture flotation machine components for mineral processing operations worldwide. Our experience includes fabricating structural and mechanical elements such as flotation tanks, stators, rotors, baffles, and launders—each designed and built to withstand aggressive slurry environments and 24/7 operation.

With over 20 years in large-scale industrial fabrication, we apply tight-tolerance machining, heavy-duty plate forming, and robotic welding to produce reliable, long-life components. We work directly from your 2D/3D drawings or collaborate with your engineering team for manufacturability improvements.

Typical Flotation Components We Fabricate

  • Tanks & Cell Bodies: Built from heavy carbon or stainless steel, with internal rubber lining or AR plate lining as required
  • Rotor-Stator Sets: Machined precisely for turbulent flow and energy efficiency
  • Baffles & Launders: Flow-diverting assemblies with coatings for slurry handling
  • Support Frames & Mounting Skids: Welded base structures with stress relief and inspection
  • Shafts, Couplings & Blades: Custom rotating assemblies machined to spec

Fabrication & Machining Capabilities

  • Cutting: Laser (up to 32mm), plasma (up to 100mm), waterjet (up to 200mm), flame (up to 500mm)
  • Bending: Press brakes up to 3,000 tons, length 15 m, plate thickness up to 100 mm
  • Rolling: Cold/hot rolling for plates up to 210 mm thick and 4250 mm wide
  • Welding: Manual, robotic, and submerged arc welding with over 230 systems
  • Machining: Horizontal boring and CNC milling for components up to 50 m × 8 m × 7 m, 250 tons
  • Surface Treatment: Blasting, epoxy coatings, rubber lining, marine-grade finishes

Material Options

  • Carbon steel (Q235B, Q345B, ASTM A36/A572)
  • Stainless steel (304, 316L, Duplex)
  • Abrasion-resistant plate (AR400, AR500)
  • Rubber-lined plate and liners
  • High-manganese steel and nickel-based alloys

Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001, ASME U Stamp, CE, API, and LR certified
  • ZEISS MMZ G and ACCURA II CMMs, Leica AT901 laser tracker
  • Weld inspection (UT, MT, PT), dimensional inspection, hardness and pressure testing
  • Complete traceability and material certificates provided with every project

Why Choose Openex

  • We specialize in complex, heavy steel components for harsh industrial environments
  • Integrated production: cutting, bending, welding, machining, and coating in one facility
  • Skilled team: Our welders, machinists, and QA staff are trained to handle critical tolerances
  • We collaborate with OEMs, EPCs, and plant operators for both new equipment and replacement parts
