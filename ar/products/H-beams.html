Custom H Beam Fabrication

Precision-Welded H Beams for Heavy Industrial Applications

Openex specializes in the fabrication of custom welded H beams tailored for high-strength, corrosion-resistant, and load-bearing applications across industries such as construction, bridges, shipbuilding, petrochemicals, offshore structures, and energy infrastructure. Our advanced production line, supported by proprietary welding fixtures and technology, ensures minimal distortion and superior alignment during the beam assembly process.

To overcome the typical welding deformation challenges faced in traditional H beam production, Openex developed a specialized H beam welding fixture system. This innovative solution uses synchronized rotating turntables with limit racks and clamping elements to hold web and flange plates firmly in position, allowing for controlled and distortion-free welding. This technology significantly improves both structural integrity and fabrication efficiency.

Manufacturing Capabilities

  • Beam production capability: Up to 5000t per month
  • Materials: Stainless steel (304, 316L), carbon steel (Q235B, Q345B), high-strength steels
  • Processes: CNC cutting, edge planing, prebending, subassembly, SAW/MIG welding, post-weld straightening
  • Surface Finishing: Blasting, priming, passivation, ultrasonic testing

Advantages of Openex H Beams

  • High strength-to-weight ratio with optimized section design
  • Reduced deformation via custom-developed welding jigs
  • Superior corrosion resistance using stainless steel and duplex materials
  • High dimensional precision with laser and blue point inspection
  • Modular welding line supports both low- and high-volume production

Quality Control

  • Ultrasonic weld inspection and blue point testing
  • Material traceability and certification (MTC)
  • Dimensional verification using laser tracker systems
  • Strict compliance with GB, EN, and AWS D1.1 standards
  • Post-fabrication stress relief and straightening as needed

Applications

  • Steel structures and bridges
  • High-rise buildings and architectural frameworks
  • Offshore and marine engineering
  • Oil & gas processing platforms
  • Heavy machinery frames and transport equipment
