Nuclear Equipment Components Fabrication

Precision-Engineered Components for Nuclear Power and Research Facilities

Openex is a trusted, certified manufacturer of heavy-duty and precision components used in nuclear power generation, nuclear fuel processing, experimental reactor systems, and nuclear research infrastructure. Our facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art fabrication equipment and a robust quality assurance system, allowing us to deliver nuclear-grade parts and assemblies that comply with the strictest safety and material traceability requirements.

Fabricated Components for the Nuclear Industry

We have successfully fabricated and delivered over 300 different types of components to nuclear facilities and EPC contractors worldwide. Our scope of supply includes but is not limited to:

  • Steam generator manifolds
  • Condenser shells and tube sheet structures
  • Reactor heads, assemblies, and dome covers
  • Nuclear vibration isolation test benches and lifting fixtures
  • Leg columns, supports, and base ring structures
  • Reactor internals: core shrouds, guide tubes, baffle plates, and control rod sleeves
  • Support assemblies, brackets, and mechanical arms
  • Flow distributors, equalization plates, and structural diffusers
  • Coolant circulation piping, large-diameter elbows, and pipelines
  • Nuclear pump motor housings and turbine exhaust shells
  • Control panel structures, stainless steel consoles, and operating decks
  • Shield blocks, biological shielding plugs, and neutron absorber panels

Fabrication Capabilities

  • Component Size: Up to 10 meters in diameter, 60 tons in unit weight
  • Machining: 5-axis CNC turning, horizontal boring (50m × 8m × 7m)
  • Welding: TIG, MIG, SAW, laser welding, and electron beam (EBW)
  • Plate Forming: Rolling of stainless and alloy plates up to 150 mm thick
  • Heat Treatment: Large-format PWHT furnace (35m × 11m × 12.5m)
  • Surface Processing: Electro-polishing, pickling, passivation, cleanroom degreasing
  • Clean Manufacturing: Class 10,000 cleanroom environment for component assembly

Materials and Metallurgical Expertise

Openex routinely works with complex alloys and specialty steels required for high radiation, corrosion, and pressure environments. These materials are sourced from certified nuclear-grade suppliers and subjected to rigorous quality checks.

  • Stainless steels: 304L, 316L, 321, 347H, 310S
  • Duplex and Super Duplex (2205, 2507) for chloride resistance
  • Low-alloy pressure vessel steels: SA-387, SA-203, SA-516
  • Nickel-based superalloys: Inconel 600/625, Hastelloy C-276, Monel 400
  • Zirconium and titanium (for advanced experimental systems)
  • Clad and explosion-bonded plates for core support assemblies

Quality Assurance, Inspection & Traceability

Openex implements a nuclear-qualified quality system aligned with NQA-1, ISO 19443, ASME Section III, and other global nuclear codes. Every step of production is tracked, recorded, and verified through an auditable documentation system.

  • WPS/PQR/WPQ documentation for every weld process
  • Full Material Traceability Reports (MTRs)
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT): RT, UT, PT, MT, PMI, VT, and ferrite content
  • Dimensional verification using portable CMMs and laser trackers
  • Cleanroom assembly inspections for reactor internals
  • Mechanical testing: tensile, impact (Charpy), hardness, intergranular corrosion
