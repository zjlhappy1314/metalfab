Rolled Cylinders Fabrication

Precision Formed Cylinders for Industrial Applications

At Openex, we specialize in the precision rolling and fabrication of steel cylinders and seamless shells used across a wide range of heavy industries — including energy, petrochemicals, pressure vessels, construction, marine, and bulk storage. With more than ten state-of-the-art plate rolling machines in operation, we offer one of the most diverse forming capacities in the region.

Our rolled cylinders are used as standalone parts or as structural bases for vessels, silos, tanks, hoppers, pipelines, and mechanical assemblies. We can deliver shell segments with or without welding depending on your requirements for material integrity and assembly.

Rolling Capabilities

  • Maximum Cylinder Length: 4250 mm (single roll)
  • Maximum Plate Thickness: Up to 200 mm
  • Maximum Cylinder Diameter: Up to 5000 mm
  • Minimum Radius: As low as 150 mm depending on thickness
  • Forming Techniques: 3-roll, 4-roll, CNC-controlled, hot and cold rolling

Materials We Roll

  • Carbon Steel: A36, Q235, Q345, A516, A572 GR50
  • Stainless Steel: 304, 316L, 309, 310
  • Aluminum: 6061 T6, 5083, 5052
  • Nickel Alloys: Inconel 625, Monel 400, Hastelloy
  • Abrasion-Resistant Plate: AR400, AR450, Hardox 450, Hardox 500
  • High Strength Low Alloy: S355, Q690D, A514

Industries and Use Cases

  • Pressure vessel and shell fabrication
  • Oil and gas piping structures
  • Chemical and water treatment tanks
  • Silo and storage drum components
  • Offshore, marine, and defense structures
  • Large ductwork, elbows, and transition cones

Welding and Finishing Options

  • Submerged Arc Welding (SAW), MIG/TIG for full cylinder seam welding
  • Beveling, edge prep, and automatic fit-up systems
  • Shot blasting and priming or full coating on request
  • Tolerance control and roundness verification with laser scanners
  • Post-forming machining of edges, bolt holes, or flanges

Certifications & Quality

  • ISO 9001 certified facility
  • Plate rolling processes compliant with ASME and EN standards
  • Welding certified per AWS D1.1, ISO 9606, EN 1090
  • In-process inspection and dimensional control with laser measurement tools
  • Material traceability and mill test certifications for all rolled plate

Why Choose Openex for Rolled Cylinders?

  • One of the largest selections of plate rolls in Asia
  • Exceptional control of diameter, roundness, and edge alignment
  • Expertise in rolling exotic and difficult-to-form metals
  • Flexible order quantities — from prototyping to batch production
  • Integrated forming, welding, machining, and QA under one roof
