Precision Fabrication of Mining & Construction Dump Truck Bodies

We provide custom dump body fabrication services for clients in the heavy equipment and mining industries, delivering high-strength, precision-built steel dump bodies for the most demanding work environments. Leveraging advanced fabrication technology and decades of experience, we supply durable and reliable dump bodies tailored to your exact specifications.

Fabrication Capabilities

Our dump bodies are built using abrasion-resistant (AR) steel plates, engineered to handle heavy loads, constant impact, and aggressive materials. Every dump body is cut, formed, welded, and finished in-house using the latest automated fabrication systems.

Core Processes:

  • CNC Cutting & Bending: High-precision shaping for structural parts
  • Robotic Welding: Consistent, strong weld seams using large automated positioners
  • Machining: Precision milling, drilling, and fitment for hinge points and hydraulic integration
  • UT Inspection: All critical welds are tested via Ultrasonic Testing (UT) for internal integrity
  • Surface Preparation: Sandblasting for clean adhesion and corrosion resistance
  • Coating: Primer + topcoat (standard yellow or custom color as specified)
