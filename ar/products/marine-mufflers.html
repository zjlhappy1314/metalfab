Custom Marine Mufflers Fabrication

Precision-Built Exhaust Silencing Systems for Marine Applications

Openex specializes in the custom fabrication of marine exhaust mufflers and silencers engineered for performance, durability, and acoustic control in demanding marine environments. Designed to handle backpressure, saltwater exposure, and high thermal loads, our mufflers are built to meet the acoustic and structural requirements of shipbuilders, naval designers, and offshore platforms.

Whether you require inline silencers for propulsion engines, generator mufflers, water-cooled configurations, or custom hybrid systems, Openex delivers heavy-duty, corrosion-resistant mufflers tailored to your vessel's specifications. From fishing fleets and ferries to offshore rigs and naval vessels, we build marine mufflers that perform reliably in the harshest conditions.

Types of Marine Mufflers We Fabricate

  • Dry mufflers for diesel engine exhaust
  • Wet exhaust mufflers with water injection ports
  • Inline and side-entry muffler configurations
  • High-performance silencers for auxiliary engines and generators
  • Engine room acoustic silencers with thermal insulation
  • Custom-designed multi-chamber reactive and absorptive mufflers

Fabrication Capabilities

  • Diameter: Up to 2.5 meters
  • Length: Up to 10 meters
  • Wall Thickness: Up to 20 mm for high-pressure systems
  • Welding: TIG, MIG, SAW with X-ray/NDT inspection
  • Assembly: Internal baffles, expansion chambers, water injection tubes
  • Surface Treatment: Marine-grade paint, galvanizing, ceramic thermal coatings

Materials We Work With

  • Stainless Steel 304, 316L, 321 for seawater and heat resistance
  • Mild steel with epoxy or ceramic coating
  • Corrosion-resistant alloys for specialty marine systems

Testing & Quality Control

  • Weld testing: RT, UT, MT
  • Dimensional verification and hydrostatic testing
  • Noise and vibration testing (available upon request)
  • Third-party inspection and classification approval support

Why Choose Openex?

  • Custom-engineered mufflers for noise, flow, and space optimization
  • Certified to ISO 9001, CE, and major marine classification standards
  • Robust QA, on-time delivery, and expert fabrication
  • Support for one-off builds, series production, and OEM partnerships
