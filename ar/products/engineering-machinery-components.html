Custom Fabrication of Engineering Machinery Components

Heavy-Duty Structural Fabrication for Construction & Mobile Equipment

Openex is a trusted manufacturer of precision-fabricated components for engineering machinery. We produce robust, high-performance structural parts including booms, arms, buckets, frames, dump bodies, racks, and custom welded assemblies for excavators, cranes, mining trucks, and other heavy equipment.

Our 160,000 m² advanced fabrication facility integrates laser/plasma cutting, CNC bending, robotic welding, large-part machining, and high-capacity painting lines. With the ability to process medium to ultra-heavy steel components, we supply ready-to-assemble structures for OEMs and global equipment manufacturers.

Components We Fabricate

  • Excavator Booms & Arms
  • Loader and Crane Buckets
  • Main Frames and Underframes
  • Truck Dump Bodies
  • Battery Racks, Mounting Brackets
  • Counterweights and Wear Structures
  • Lift Arms, Swing Frames, Chassis Structures

Industries & Equipment Served

  • Construction Machinery (Excavators, Cranes, Loaders)
  • Mining Trucks & Earthmoving Equipment
  • Heavy-Duty Trailers & Transport Vehicles
  • Hydraulic Equipment & Attachments
  • Industrial Lifting & Material Handling Machines
  • Energy, Port Machinery, and Infrastructure Equipment

Fabrication Capabilities

  • Laser, plasma, and flame cutting for thick and hardened plates
  • Plate bending up to 3,000 tons and 15 meters
  • MIG, TIG, submerged arc, and robotic welding
  • CNC machining for precision fit-up and bore tolerances
  • Blasting, coating, and paint systems (primer + finish coats)
  • Sub-assembly, kitting, and load-ready packing

Materials We Work With

  • Carbon Steel (Q235, Q345, ASTM A36/A572)
  • High Strength Steel (HARDOX, DOMEX, Strenx)
  • Abrasion-Resistant Plate (AR400/AR500)
  • Alloy Steels (42CrMo, 20MnTiB)
  • Stainless Steels and Aluminum Alloys (on request)

Quality Assurance

  • Certified welding procedures (ISO, AWS)
  • Non-destructive testing: UT, MT, PT
  • Dimensional inspection and CMM verification
  • Load testing and weld seam evaluation (upon request)
  • Full traceability of materials and production records

Why Choose Openex?

  • Large Part Handling: Workpieces up to 250 tons and 50 meters in length
  • Flexible Production: Prototypes, low-volume, or mass production supported
  • Global Delivery: Exported to Asia, Europe, Middle East, and Americas
