Large Pressure Vessel & Industrial Tank Fabrication

Heavy, High-Pressure Industrial Vessels Engineered for Performance

Openex delivers engineered pressure vessels and large-capacity storage tanks for the most demanding industrial environments. Built in-house, our vessels meet global standards such as ASME, API, and GB/T, and are deployed across oil & gas, chemical, refining, power generation, and process manufacturing sectors.

With capabilities to fabricate vessels up to 8 meters in diameter, 100 meters in length, and 1,000 tons per unit, we are among the few manufacturers globally capable of handling ultra-large cylindrical and modular pressure vessel assemblies. Our expertise covers thick-wall pressure-bearing shells, multi-chamber reactors, high-pressure separators, and custom shell-and-tube bodies.

Our Vessel Range Includes:

  • High-pressure horizontal & vertical vessels
  • Refinery reactors, columns, scrubbers, separators
  • Heat exchanger shells and cooling towers
  • Storage tanks for chemicals, fuels, hydrogen, ammonia
  • Skid-mounted modular pressure systems

Manufacturing Capabilities

  • Max Diameter: 8 meters
  • Max Length: 100 meters
  • Max Unit Weight: 1,000 tons
  • Shell Forming: Cold and hot plate rolling, cone forming
  • Welding: SAW, MIG, TIG, robotic, orbital, and strip cladding
  • Heat Treatment: PWHT, annealing, normalizing (furnace: 35m × 11m × 12.5m)
  • Surface Treatment: Internal linings, anti-corrosion paint, rubber/FRP lining, thermal spray coatings

Material Capabilities

  • Carbon Steel: Q345R, SA516 Gr. 60/65/70
  • Alloy Steels: SA387 Gr. 11/22, Cr-Mo-V alloys
  • Stainless Steel: 304, 316L, 321, 904L, Duplex, Super Duplex
  • Nickel-Based Alloys: Inconel, Hastelloy, Monel
  • Clad Plates & Explosion-Bonded Materials

Inspection & Quality Control

  • Non-destructive Testing (UT, RT, MT, PT, PMI)
  • Hydrostatic & Pneumatic Pressure Testing
  • Weld Qualification: WPS/PQR, third-party witnessed
  • Dimensional Control: 3D laser tracker, CMM
  • Mechanical Tests: Impact, hardness, tensile
  • Full Traceability: MTRs, NDE records, heat maps

Why Choose Openex?

  • One-stop manufacturing from steel plate to finished pressure vessel
  • Certified under ASME (U, U2), ISO 9001, CE, API
  • In-house engineering support with design verification and FEA validation
  • Global delivery and packaging for modular system integration
