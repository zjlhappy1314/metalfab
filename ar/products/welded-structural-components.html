Welded Structural Components Fabrication

High-Precision Welded Structures for Heavy Industry

Openex specializes in the fabrication of welded structural components for a wide range of sectors including construction equipment, mining, energy, marine, heavy machinery, and infrastructure. Our facilities are equipped to handle large welded assemblies with strict dimensional and strength requirements, leveraging over two decades of experience in fabricating robust structural frames, assemblies, platforms, and enclosures.

Whether it's high-load structural frames for cranes, reinforced chassis for mining trucks, or offshore platforms with extreme fatigue resistance, Openex delivers welded structures that meet both mechanical integrity and international fabrication standards.

Typical Products and Structures

  • Base frames and skid assemblies for heavy equipment
  • Machinery platforms, walkways, staircases, and structural racks
  • Structural members and lattice structures for cranes and booms
  • Engineered supports, saddles, and transportation fixtures
  • Truck frames, dump bodies, and undercarriage structures
  • Industrial foundation frames and building supports

Capabilities Overview

  • Component Weight: Up to 100 tons per assembly
  • Welding: MIG/MAG, TIG, SAW, FCAW, robotic and semi-automatic welding
  • Plate Forming: Bending capacity up to 3000 tons, 15m long, thickness up to 100 mm
  • Machining: Large-scale CNC gantry milling, boring, turning
  • Surface Prep: Blasting, priming, painting, hot-dip galvanizing

Materials We Handle

  • Carbon steel: Q235B, Q345B, S355, ASTM A36
  • High-strength low-alloy (HSLA): S690QL, 16Mn
  • Abrasion-resistant plate: HARDOX, NM400/500
  • Stainless steels: 304, 316L, Duplex grades
  • Weathering steel (Corten) and marine-grade alloys

Welding Certifications and QA

  • Certified welders per AWS
  • Welding Procedure Specifications (WPS) and Procedure Qualification Records (PQR)
  • Non-destructive testing (NDT): UT, RT, MT, PT
  • Dimensional inspection with 3D laser tracker and CMM
  • Load and fit-up test jigs for frame assemblies

Industries Served

  • Heavy construction and mining equipment
  • Oil and gas modules and subsea structures
  • Wind and solar energy support frames
  • Shipbuilding and offshore engineering
  • Rail, military, and infrastructure components

Why Openex?

  • Proven capability in fabricating large, complex structural modules
  • Strict quality control and traceability for critical welds
  • On-time delivery for domestic and international clients
  • Experienced team with project management and engineering support
