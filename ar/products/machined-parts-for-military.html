Custom Fabrication of Heavy Machined Components for Military Applications

Mission-Ready Manufacturing for Defense-Grade Components

Openex provides precision fabrication and heavy machining services for critical military components. With over two decades of experience in high-strength steel processing and ultra-large-format part machining, we serve defense contractors and system integrators requiring structural, mechanical, and armor-grade assemblies.

From ground combat vehicle components to naval structural assemblies and launch support equipment, we deliver tight-tolerance, certified metal components capable of withstanding extreme environments and operational loads.

Defense Components We Manufacture

  • Chassis parts and hull segments for military vehicles
  • Bases, bearing housings, and recoil assemblies
  • Mounts machined from hardened steel
  • Frames, base plates, and skids
  • Heavy-duty fixtures, braces, and mobility support structures

Fabrication & Machining Capabilities

  • Machining: Up to 50 m × 8 m × 7 m work envelope, handling weights up to 250 tons
  • Welding: Certified MIG, TIG, and SAW welding for structural, armor, and pressure-rated joints
  • Cutting: Laser, plasma, waterjet, and oxy-fuel cutting up to 500 mm thick
  • Bending/Forming: 3,000-ton presses with 15 m bending length and 100 mm thickness capacity
  • Inspection: CMM, laser tracker, UT, MT, PT, and full dimensional reporting
  • Surface Treatment: Blasting, MIL-spec paint, anti-corrosion coatings, powder coating

Commonly Used Materials

  • Armor-grade steel (MIL-A-12560, MIL-DTL-46100)
  • Carbon steel (A572 Gr. 50, Q345B, A36)
  • High-strength alloy steels (4340, 8620)
  • Stainless steels (304, 316L, 17-4PH)
  • Aluminum alloys (5083, 6061, 7075)

Certifications & Quality Control

  • Certifications: ISO 9001, ASME, CE, API
  • QA Processes: Third-party witnessed testing, documentation packages
  • Traceability: Full MTRs, heat treatment records, weld maps, and process logs

Why Openex for Military Fabrication?

  • Extensive experience in defense-grade machining and fabrication
  • Integrated production flow: From plate cutting to final inspection under one roof
  • Proven track record: Delivered structural assemblies and support systems for land, sea, and missile platforms
  • Confidentiality assured: ITAR-ready processes and secure data handling
