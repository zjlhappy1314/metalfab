Machining and Metal Fabrication For Energy


We offer advanced solutions for the manufacture of key components for different equipment used for power generation in the wind energy, nuclear energy, gas and hydroelectric power sectors.

We have solid experience in the most advanced manufacturing processes and in the development of specific equipment that ensure precision and quality required in the energy sector.

We provide custom solutions for high-value-added components such as hubs, frames, components of speed multipliers, nacelle, bodies and shafts of turbines, generators, compressors, inner & outer casings, rotor housing, nuclear components, planetary gears, tower, ring, etc.

Our Full-Service Metal Fabrication: metal cutting and sheering, metal forming, bending, and rolling, welding, CNC machining, sheet metal assembly, and quality inspection

Machining Services: Multi-Axis Machining, 5-Axis Machining, CNC Milling | Vertical and Horizontal, CNC Turning | Vertical and Horizontal, CNC Engineering, CMM Inspection

Material Types: castings, forgings, fabricated weldments, billets, bar stock, and plate

Metal Materials we worked: Stainless steel, Carbon Steel, aluminum, brass, bronze, chromium, copper, Monel, nickel, titanium, zinc, and many other superalloys.

Certifications: ISO 9001, 14001,18001, CE EN 1090-1, ISO 3834, ABS, AWS, API, ASME, DNV.GL.

Industries Served: Mining, Construction, Oil & Gas, Power Generation, Marine, Automotive, Renewable Energy, Pulp & Paper, Bridge, Aerospace, Defense, Printing and Dyeing, Machinery, etc.
