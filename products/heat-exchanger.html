Industrial Heat Exchanger Fabrication

Engineered Heat Transfer Components Built to Perform

Openex Metal Fabrication manufactures custom heat exchanger assemblies for demanding industrial applications, including petrochemical, power generation, marine, and heavy processing environments. We build shell-and-tube exchangers, plate-fin units, and pressure-rated vessels to exact customer designs — integrating precision machining, certified welding, and rigorous quality control.

From large-scale fabricated headers and tube sheets to complete skid-mounted exchangers, we offer full-service production capabilities in-house. With certified welding procedures (ASME, AWS), heavy-duty rolling and forming equipment, and state-of-the-art CNC machining centers, Openex is your one-stop partner for heat exchanger fabrication at scale.

Custom Fabricated Components

  • Shell & Tube Exchangers: Pressure-rated designs with seamless integration of tube bundles and shell-side features
  • Tube Sheets & Baffles: CNC-drilled to ±0.05 mm with leak-tested welds
  • Heads, Nozzles & Channels: Forged or fabricated with flanged or integrally welded connections
  • Plates & Panels: Flat and formed plates for air-cooled and plate-type exchangers
  • Complete Assemblies: Skid-mounted and pre-tested exchanger modules for direct system installation

Fabrication & Engineering Capabilities

  • Tube Sheet Drilling: CNC precision with ±0.05 mm tolerance
  • Welding: Manual and orbital welding for shell, tube, and baffle joints (ASME-certified)
  • Rolling & Forming: Up to 210 mm plate thickness and 4250 mm width
  • Machining: Horizontal and vertical boring, facing, and profiling
  • Cutting: Laser, plasma, waterjet, and flame methods — thick plate and contour components
  • Testing: RT, UT, hydrostatic, pneumatic, and helium leak detection

Materials We Work With

  • Carbon Steel (A106, Q235B, Q345R)
  • Stainless Steel (304, 316L, 321, Duplex)
  • Nickel Alloys (Inconel, Hastelloy, Monel)
  • Copper-Nickel (90/10, 70/30)
  • Aluminum, Titanium (upon request)

Certifications & Quality Assurance

  • ISO 9001, CE, ASME U Stamp, API, Lloyd’s Register certified
  • ZEISS MMZ G CMM, Leica AT901 laser tracker for dimensional verification
  • PMI, pressure test, and NDT reports included per project spec
  • Full material traceability and fabrication documentation

Why Openex?

  • In-house integration: Cutting, forming, welding, machining, coating, testing
  • Built for scale: Components and systems up to 250 tons in weight
  • Responsive to EPCs, OEMs, and plant operators worldwide
