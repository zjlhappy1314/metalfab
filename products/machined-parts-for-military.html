Custom Fabrication of Heavy Machined Components for Military Applications

Mission-Ready Manufacturing for Defense-Grade Components

Openex provides precision fabrication and heavy machining services for critical military components. With over two decades of experience in high-strength steel processing and ultra-large-format part machining, we serve defense contractors and system integrators requiring structural, mechanical, and armor-grade assemblies.

From ground combat vehicle components to naval structural assemblies and launch support equipment, we deliver tight-tolerance, certified metal components capable of withstanding extreme environments and operational loads.

Defense Components We Manufacture

  • Chassis parts and hull segments for military vehicles
  • Bases, bearing housings, and recoil assemblies
  • Mounts machined from hardened steel
  • Frames, base plates, and skids
  • Heavy-duty fixtures, braces, and mobility support structures
