Overview of Large-diameter flanges

Large-diameter flanges are essential connection components used in pipelines, pressure vessels, heat exchangers, and heavy-duty mechanical systems across industries such as oil & gas, power generation, petrochemical, marine, and offshore platforms. We specialize in the custom manufacturing of high-integrity large flanges—engineered to handle extreme pressure, temperature, and corrosive environments.

Manufacturing Capabilities

Our large flange production facilities are equipped with high-capacity CNC lathes, vertical turning centers, and boring mills to handle diameters up to 10,000 mm (392 inches) and weights exceeding 50 tons.

Machining and Dimensions

  • Size Range: Up to 10,000 mm OD (outer diameter)
  • Thickness: 640mm
  • Facing Types: RF (Raised Face), FF (Flat Face), RTJ (Ring-Type Joint), MF (Male-Female), TG (Tongue & Groove)
  • Bore and Groove Machining: Tapered or parallel bore as per custom specs

    • Flange Types We Manufacture

  • Weld Neck (WN)
  • Blind (BL)
  • Slip-On (SO)
  • Long Weld Neck (LWN)
  • Lap Joint (LJ)
  • Threaded (TH)
  • Orifice Flanges
  • Custom Segmental Flanges (multi-piece for oversized transport)

    • Welding & Fabrication

  • Welded Construction – Multi-segment welded flanges available for sizes exceeding single-piece forging capacity
  • Heat Treatment – Normalizing, stress relieving, quenching & tempering, and solution annealing available per specification

    • Testing & Quality Control

    Each flange undergoes comprehensive inspection and testing based on client requirements and applicable codes:
    Dimensional Verification: CMM or laser measurement for high-precision components
    Non-Destructive Testing (NDT):

  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT)
  • Magnetic Particle Testing (MT)
  • Dye Penetrant Testing (PT)
  • Radiographic Testing (RT) on welds

  • Subsea and offshore platforms
  • Oil & gas transmission pipelines
  • LNG and refinery units
  • Steam and gas turbines
  • Nuclear and fossil fuel power plants
  • High-pressure chemical reactors
  • Industrial water systems and cooling towers
