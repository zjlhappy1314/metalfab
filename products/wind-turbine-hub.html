Wind Turbine Hub Casting and Machining

Heavy-Duty Hub Fabrication for Wind Energy Systems

Openex is a trusted manufacturer of large-scale wind turbine hubs, offering a complete solution from mold design and casting to high-precision CNC machining, surface finishing, and quality assurance. Our manufacturing capabilities support both onshore and offshore wind power projects with components engineered to endure high dynamic loads, vibration, and environmental exposure.

Our wind turbine hubs — weighing up to 40 tons — are cast from premium ductile iron or alloyed steel, heat-treated for structural integrity, and machined to tight tolerances for seamless nacelle integration. Each hub is inspected and certified to meet global OEM and EPC specifications.

Complete Hub Manufacturing Process

  1. Mold Design & Casting: Advanced pattern-making and controlled pouring for structural uniformity
  2. Heat Treatment: Stress relieving and normalizing to enhance material toughness
  3. Rough & Finish Machining: Multi-axis CNC turning and boring up to 6 m diameter
  4. Drilling & Tapping: High-precision bolt hole placement with thread validation
  5. Dynamic Balancing: To ensure rotational performance and reduce nacelle stress
  6. Coating: Anti-corrosive primer, topcoat, and customized protective finishes

Technical Capabilities

  • Hub Weight Capacity: Up to 40 tons per casting
  • Machining Tolerance: ±0.01 mm on critical interfaces
  • Max Turning Diameter: 6 meters
  • Surface Coating: marine-grade coating system for offshore applications

Other Wind Components We Produce

  • Precision Flanges: For tower-tube connections and nacelle base flanges
  • Tower Segments: Cut, rolled, welded, and blasted steel shell segments
  • Monopiles & Pile Tips: Heavy-wall offshore foundations
  • Suction Buckets: Fabricated and machined for subsea installation
  • Transition Pieces & Platforms: Structural nodes and bracing elements

Inspection & Quality Assurance

  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT) & Radiographic Testing (RT) for internal flaw detection
  • Magnetic Particle (MT) and Liquid Penetrant (PT) surface testing
  • 3D CMM inspection & laser scanning for geometric accuracy
  • Mechanical property validation (tensile, impact, Brinell hardness)
  • Material traceability and full MTR documentation

  • Proven experience delivering to leading wind OEMs and EPCs
  • Capacity for high-volume, high-mix production of wind energy structures
  • Compliance with ISO 9001, CE, and wind industry-specific QA standards
