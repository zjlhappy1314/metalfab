Custom Fabrication for Lithium Battery Production Equipment

Precision Structures and Components for Energy Manufacturing

Openex provides full-service metal fabrication for the lithium battery manufacturing industry, producing structural assemblies, enclosures, containers, and equipment frames for battery production lines. With experience supporting large-scale battery projects, we deliver high-quality steel and alloy components built to precise dimensional and surface standards.

Our production scope covers fabrication, welding, machining, surface treatment, and assembly — delivering turnkey solutions to lithium battery equipment integrators and OEMs. We fabricate from mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and nickel alloys, ensuring structural integrity and chemical compatibility for the application.

Components & Assemblies We Fabricate

  • Main structural parts: columns, large platforms, intermediate platforms, small platforms
  • Load-bearing and safety parts: short beams, staircases, steel ladders, handrails
  • Enclosures & casings: nickel boxes, protective housings, battery container shells
  • Battery system assemblies: battery box bodies, support frames, mixing tanks
  • Production line steelwork: structural bases and anchor points for automated lithium production systems
  • Storage & handling units: material bins, buffer tanks, base plates for battery containers

Fabrication & Process Capabilities

  • Cutting: Laser, waterjet, flame, and plasma cutting for precision profiles
  • Bending & forming: Up to 3,000 tons, 15 meters long, 100 mm thick material
  • Welding: Robotic and manual MIG, TIG, SAW with certified welding procedures
  • Machining: High-accuracy boring, milling, and drilling for component integration
  • Surface Finishing: Sandblasting, powder coating, galvanizing, and pickling/passivation
  • Assembly: Subframe installation, functional mock-up, and factory fit checks

Materials We Commonly Use

  • Carbon steel (Q235B, Q345B)
  • Stainless steel (304, 316L)
  • Nickel and nickel alloys
  • Aluminum and alloyed aluminum
  • Powder-coated and pre-treated structural steel

Industries Served

  • Lithium battery cell and pack manufacturers
  • EV battery module production lines
  • Energy storage system (ESS) suppliers
  • Battery material processing plants
  • Automation equipment integrators

Why Openex?
