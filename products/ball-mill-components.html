Engineering Challenges & Solutions

Manufacturing segmented ball mill shells is a complex task that pushes the limits of forming and machining. We overcame challenges in:

  • Precision Hole Drilling: Multi-axis heads allowed perfect alignment of closely spaced bolt holes
  • Thermal Stability: Controlled feed rates and step machining minimized heat distortion
  • Runout Control: On-machine measurement and real-time adjustments ensured concentricity
  • Weight Management: Components over 90 tons handled using custom supports and safety protocols

Inspection & Quality Assurance

  • Coordinate Measuring Machine (CMM) inspection
  • Ultrasonic testing (UT) of weld seams
  • Dimensional verification with laser tracking systems
  • Surface finish analysis and hardness testing
