Custom Welding Services

Openex provides premium metal and welding services, ensuring superior weld joint and edge quality for diverse metal fabrication needs and projects. Our experienced welders utilize cutting-edge welding equipment and techniques to deliver exceptional results that meet industry standards.

Our team of AWS welders is experienced in freehand welding of metal parts fabricated from aluminum, mild steel, and stainless steel. While most work is with machine OEM assemblies and electrical enclosures, our welders are comfortable with a wide variety of sheet metal and structural steel welded products.

Bolster your application with precision welding services from Openex. By combining unmatched technical expertise with a relentless problem-solving focus, we deliver innovative and market-leading fabrication solutions for critical jobs. The welding and fabrication processes open many manufacturing doors when concrete solutions are needed for customers. A product is created entirely from raw materials by utilizing metal fabrication, including manufacturing processes such as welding, CNC milling, and others. The designer or creator has complete control over the finished product, making custom pieces possible and able to be built to fit the exact need every time.

Our experienced and highly skilled welders are dedicated to performing high-value welding on challenging applications. Overcoming Welding Hurdles

Working with certified welders is the best way to handle your welding needs, but you'll run into hurdles if you don't collaborate with reliable professionals. While welders can typically present a certificate that proves their skills, this documentation won't always guarantee excellent service.

Some welders may lack the experience manufacturers require to create intricate or application-specific components. Others may make the right final product but can't achieve it on schedule. At Openex, our team of experienced welders offers the productivity and skill required to complete your vision according to your project timeline.
