Metal Plate Cutting Service

High precision metal cutting is one of our core services. We emerged as a metal fabricator leader providing high-quality, efficiency, speed, and unparalleled customer service.

Today, our CNC metal cutting service offers the most consistent and accurate cuts, with the highest cut qualities. Material thickness, metal type, and shape usually dictate the optimum cutting process. Rest assured, we have the perfect cutting solution for you! Openex is capable of handling material up to 10,000 pounds and 60 feet in length.

Our Cutting Capabilities

Ours CNC laser cutting service features a state-of-the-art high-precision laser to produce high-speed, precision laser-cut custom parts. Our laser cutter cuts thicker materials, including mild steel, aluminum, and stainless steel.
Cutting Method: Laser cutting
Specialty: Thin Complex Plate
Advantages: High efficiency, High speed, High accuracy
Laser Cutting Capability Max: thickness 32mm width 4100mm length 32000mm

Plasma cutting is performed by releasing gas at high speeds from a plasma torch where an electrical current is applied to turn gas into plasma arc. The plasma is used to pierce the steel and cut.
Our high precision cnc plasma cutting service producing high quality plasma cut parts by using state-of-the-art plasma cutters. Int is a very cost-effective, precise method to cut complex shapes out of the metal plate cleanly. Our plasma cutter cuts thicker materials, including steel, mild steel, aluminum, and stainless steel.
Cutting Method: Plasma cutting
Specialty: Medium Plate
Advantages: High Accuracy, High Speed, Improve heating deformation by cutting underwater
Plasma Cutting Capability Max: thickness 100mm width 4500mm length 20000mm

25 sets of leading water jet cutting machines allow us to produce complex shapes, to extremely tight tolerances, in a broad range of materials such as aluminum, steel and copper.
Cutting Method: Water Jet cutting
Specialty: Thick Plate (Almost all materials)
Advantages: High Accuracy, Smooth Section, No Deformation, Thick plate
Water Jet Cutting Capability Max: thickness 200mm width 4010mm length 12000mm

Flame, or oxy-fuel, cutting is an effective and efficient way to cut your metal to size and shape. The process uses a torch that combines heated oxygen and fuel gases to melt and remove steel, which is highly effective for large steel products like beams, plates, jumbo tubing, and more. Flame cutting is also the most cost-efficient method for large-scale cutting needs.
Cutting Method: Flame cutting
Specialty: Thick Carbon Steel Plate
Advantages: Low Cost, High Speed, Thick plate
Flame Cutting Capability Max: thickness 500mm width 8000mm length 24000mm
