Metal Plate Rolling Services

Metal sheet and plate rolling play an essential part in metal fabrication. Metal plate rolling is a process used to turn flat sheet or plate into cylindrical or conical shapes for future fabrication at your facility. Plate rolls typically use a series of hardened precision rolls utilizing either hydraulic or mechanical power.

Openex is one of the most capable heavy plate rolling companies and it starts with our team of expert roll operators, who have over 30 combined years of rolling experience. With 10 heavy plate rolling machines, our team has the equipment and tools they need to roll the largest and most challenging cylinders in the steel industry. During the steel plate rolling services process, our company is dedicated to providing durable and custom rolled steel plate for your specific project. The end result is outstanding quality and performance.

You Should Know

A fabrication technique, metal rolling creates rolled components from sheet or plate metal. Plate metal varies in thickness and can be challenging to manipulate into rolled parts. The ability to produce rolled fabrications requires specialized equipment to meet exacting standards. Metal rolling is a process that utilizes a number of techniques, including sheet metal rolling and cone rolling. With the ability to produce complex components, plate rolling is an invaluable fabrication process that demands precision and accuracy. Openex employs state-of-the-art equipment and expert fabricators to create metal rolled parts.
