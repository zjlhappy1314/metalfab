Who We Are

Openex Metal Fabrication is a custom industrial fabricator offering a full range of contract manufacturing services. Openex has built a solid reputation as a high-quality contract manufacturer, and industrial fabricator with the capability of manufacturing large components and sub-assemblies since the Openex's founding.

We specialize in the fabrication of custom-engineered equipment to our customer specifications. We regularly manufacture complex, large-scale industrial components, and equipment including pressure vessels, tube sheets, industrial furnaces, power generation equipment, vacuum chambers, energy storage systems, and more.

Our team includes highly skilled and experienced project managers, fitters, welders, machinists, and quality assurance personnel who work together to meet our customers' quality and delivery requirements.

We build what O.E.M. and engineering firms design and help them find real-world solutions to their industrial fabrication needs. We fabricate, machine, blast, paint, assemble, test, and ship engineered equipment according to specifications.

Our passion is building large, complex industrial equipment. We consistently deliver exceptional value by applying our expert craftsmanship, experienced project management, and innovative solutions to our customers' diverse manufacturing needs.

We are ISO, CE, ASME, API, LR certified companies. Our dedicated and professional quality control team guarantees our products meet the highest standards.

COMPONENTS & EQUIPMENT MANUFACTURED BY Openex Metal Fabrication: Pressure vessels, Tube Sheets, Condensers, Industrial Duct Works, Vacuum Chambers, Dump Body, Engineered Bases & Frames, Metal Enclosures, Mezzanines and Structural Bases, Battery Racking, Ladles, Charge Buckets, Tank Heads and More

Contact us at sales3@openex.com.cn about your next project. Openex Metal Fabrication has been fabricating large-scale, complex industrial components and machinery over 20 years. We've built a reputation for delivering the highest quality work on time and within budget.

Our Services

METAL FABRICATION

Openex Metal Fabrication offers complete turnkey fabrication and assembly for projects that range in size from moderate to epic.

CNC MACHINING

Large-scale, high-precision machining services with advanced equipment and experienced technicians.

METAL PLATE CUTTING

We can cut metals to almost any size or shape — from delicate sheets of copper to thick carbon-steel I-beams. No matter the scale, we've got the machines and staff of experienced operators to meet nearly any specification.

METAL PLATE ROLLING

Openex is one of the most capable heavy plate rolling companies and it starts with our team of expert roll operators, who have over 20 combined years of rolling experience.

WELDING

As a leader in AWS-certified welding, we offer manual and robotic welding processes to meet your application's unique needs.

PRESS BRAKING

From small intricate bends on light materials through heavy bending on materials 100mm thick up to 15 meters in length, Openex Metal Fabrication Can provide a wide variety of Press Braking Services to our customers.

PAINTING & SANDBLASTING

Our coating service department is anchored by three large, state-of-the-art steel grit blasting booths and three paint booths.

ASSSEMBLY

Get your metal fabricated assemblies from our state-of-the-art facility. Our team of highly skilled technicians has years of experience in fabricating and assembling metal components.

Industries We Serve

Mining

Mining and mineral processing requires exceptionally tough equipment. Openex is a custom steel fabrication specialist for mining and engineering. We have earned an excellent reputation in the industry for the quality of the steel and alloy products we build. We understand that high-quality workmanship, meeting delivery schedules, and value are important to our customers in the mining industry.

Oil & Gas

Oil and gas exploration, processing and delivery requires durable, hardworking equipment. We partner with you to make it possible, with full-service manufacturing capabilities and expertise throughout the process—upstream, midstream and downstream.

Power Generation

We offer advanced fabrication solutions for manufacturing key precision and quality metal components used for power generation in the wind energy, nuclear energy, gas, and hydroelectric power sectors.

Marine

Marine equipment has to withstand extreme environments. And saltwater is just the beginning. At Openex, we've built some of the biggest metal parts for the marine industry and delivered them worldwide on the water.

Automotive

When automotive manufacturers need custom metal parts and components for their vehicles, Openex has been here to fill that need for the last 20 years. We have manufactured automotive parts with precise CNC Machining Services and Sheet Metal Fabrication Services to help meet the demands of the increased market for automotive parts.

Bridge

Openex is a fully-equipped metal fabrication shop with a tradition of producing high-quality products and providing outstanding customer service. We possess the ability to fabricate large steel structures and have expanded our capabilities to include the fabrication of steel bridges and other transportation infrastructure.

Pulp and Papermaking

Openex offers steel fabrication services for pulp and paper mills. We are well equipped to provide the pulp and paper industry with the steel parts necessary for business. Steel fabrication for the parts, products, and components used in pulp and paper mills.

Aerospace

We are a leading aerospace parts manufacturer specializing in complex metal formed parts. We produce aircraft engine nacelles, bulkheads, heat shields, duct sections, and engine components.
