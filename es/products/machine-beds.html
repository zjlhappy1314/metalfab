Custom Machine Bed Manufacturing

Precision-Fabricated Machine Beds and Structures for Machine Tools

We specialize in the custom manufacturing of machine beds, uprights, gantries, and other high-precision structures used in machine tool applications. Whether it’s a casted or welded base, every component we produce is crafted to support high rigidity, long-term dimensional stability, and μ-level machining precision.

What We Offer

  • Custom Machine Beds
  • Vertical Uprights & Gantries
  • Precision Welded & Cast Components
  • Small and Large Structural Parts per Drawing

We provide complete in-house fabrication, including rough machining, high-precision finishing, weld inspection, and coating — all under strict environmental and quality controls.

Advanced Machining Capabilities

Our machining operations are conducted in a fully enclosed, 8,000 m² temperature-controlled facility, purpose-built to eliminate environmental factors that affect machining accuracy and measurement reliability.

Key Highlights:

  • μ-level machining tolerance
  • Multi-axis high-efficiency CNC systems
  • Capability for both small and ultra-large structural parts
  • 70+ machines including gantry mills, vertical lathes, boring machines, and horizontal machining centers

Representative Equipment Includes:

  • Schiess Gantry Boring & Milling Center: 7-axis, 5-face machining, max length 50m
  • WZ Heavy Turning-Milling Compound Centers: Max Ø12.5m, height 6m, load 400 tons
  • 22m Turning-Milling Center: Ø22m, height 6.5m, 650-ton load, ideal for large energy/pressure equipment
  • Innse Deep Hole Drills (Italy): Drilling depth up to 1.2m, hole Ø12–55mm, for plate thickness up to 1200mm
  • DOOYANG Multi-Axis Drilling Centers (Korea): 4-axis motion, integrated milling, drilling, tapping, boring
